About Us

Welcome to ElectroHub, your number one source for all things electronics. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of gadgets, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2020 by Tarun Kumar, ElectroHub has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Tarun first started out, his passion for eco-friendly electronics drove him to do tons of research so that ElectroHub can offer you the world's most advanced gadgets. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
